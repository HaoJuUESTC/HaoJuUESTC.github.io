A Brief Introduction

I am currently a senior undergraduate at School of Electronic Engineering, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China. I am working in Prof. Jianyu Yang’s Radar System and Signal Processing group as a research assistant under the supervision of Prof. Wei Yi, investigating Real-Time Target Detection and Estimation based on XGBoost as my undergraduate thesis. My research interests cover a broad range of subjects, including Signal & Image Processing and Human Computer Interaction, and especially, their application in the context of clinical medicine and education.

I was a former Mitacs Intern at McGill University. I worked at Shared Reality Lab, supervised by Prof. Jeremy Cooperstock. Our research designing and investigating the usability of a multi-functional foot-based musical interface went later as a paper now under review for Designing Interactive Systems (DIS) 2018.
